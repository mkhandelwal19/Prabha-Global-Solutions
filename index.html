Our Story

Founded in 2013, Prabha Global Solutions has emerged as a trusted partner for organizations seeking to harness the power of technology for competitive advantage. From our headquarters in Washington DC, we serve clients across the globe, specializing in cloud transformation, cybersecurity, and enterprise application services that drive measurable business outcomes.
